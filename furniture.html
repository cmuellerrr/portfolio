Furniture Design

I like working with my hands. I'm also sick of crappy Ikea furniture. It seemed pretty natural to combine those two things, so I started designing and building my own furniture. It's primarily woodworking, but I've done a bit of welding on some projects. Here are a few of the things I've made.

I made this solid Ash side table for some very good friends of mine. The details on the bottom are just happy accidents.

I got my hands on some amazing reclaimed Redwood from NASA's Hangar One, so I welded up a coffee table.

This is a chair that also turns into a step ladder. It's not comfortable.

This is just a basic table. I put my microwave on top of this.
