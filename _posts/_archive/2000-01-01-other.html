Better than Freddy?

Better than Freddy is the result of a conversation with my friend Jeff about whether or not a movie is "watchable". You enter the title of a movie and it tells you if it is better or worse than Freddy Got Fingered.

Check it out live.

Transcription Sucks

Sometimes you need to transcribe interviews with users. Sometimes a lot of them. Having to do this sucks. I couldn't find any simple tools that combined audio manipulation and text entry, so I built my own. This was a life saver during grad school and a hit with classmates.

Check it out live.

Human Motion Analysis

Team: Ray Liaw, Josh Caputo, Seungmoon Song, Yuzi Nakamura, Omri Amarilio

We used CMU's motion capture lab to capture test subjects performing a number of different actions (running, throwing, etc.). We then analyzed that data to try to quantify the difference in how someone throws with their dominant hand as opposed to their non-dominant hand.
