Overview

I was given the opportunity to work with the NASA Ames Research Center in order to maximize crew member efficiency while aboard the International Space Station. Given that the ISS is scheduled for decommission in 2020, we are also focused on how future missions could be improved as well. For full details on the project, please visit our team website.
