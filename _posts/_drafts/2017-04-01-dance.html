Summary

The general concept is that the app teaches people a variety of stupid dance moves; it's like Duolingo but for making you look dumb.

Keep reading for more details

Basics

The app has four main tabs: learn, moves, store, and profile.

Image of each tab name in a grid followed by bullets of their purpose.

The learn tab is where you go to learn new moves. Every move is based off of a stance.

gif of the first time you're introduced to a new stance.

Users proceed through the learn tab in stages. Each stage has you learn a few moves across a particular stance, followed by a chance to check your skills.

gif of leaning a new move.

If you want to browse through the moves you already know, you can use the moves tab.

gif of selecting a stance and then a move on the moves tab.

The store and profile tabs are pretty straightforward. You can buy access to new stances and movesets, as well as powerups to increase various stats.

Image of store and profile.
